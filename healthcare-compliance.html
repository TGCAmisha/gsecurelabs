Overview

Healthcare cyber is uniquely hard

Connected medical devices, EHR sprawl, third-party care providers, and a 24x7 operational tempo create a compliance landscape unlike any other industry.

Regulations We Cover

Frameworks our healthcare practice operates against

HIPAA Security Rule

Risk analysis, administrative, physical, and technical safeguards — including audit-ready documentation.

HITRUST CSF

r2 and i1 readiness, validation, and certification support across the full HITRUST control set.

NHS DSPT

Data Security and Protection Toolkit submission readiness for NHS-aligned organisations.

HDS / France

Hébergeur de Données de Santé certification readiness for hosting French health data.

FDA / IEC 80001

Medical device cyber security, risk management, and SBOM practices for regulated equipment.
